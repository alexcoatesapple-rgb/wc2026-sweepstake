## BUGS

### B1 — 3rd-placed teams prematurely + stickily eliminated (MEDIUM)
[deriveFromStandings](src/App.jsx:218) marks **any** non-advanced team in a
*completed* group as eliminated. But in WC26's 48-team format the **best 8
third-placed teams advance**, and that isn't decided until **all 12 groups
finish**. So when a group completes early (as today — groups A–F are done, G–L
aren't), its 3rd-placed team is marked out via ESPN's not-yet-final `advanced`
flag. Worse, [mergeDerived](src/App.jsx:240) is additive (never clears), so the
elimination is **sticky** — it survives even after ESPN later flips `advanced` to
true, and a manual ✕-untoggle is re-clobbered on the next sync.
**Confirmed live today:** the derivation eliminates `kor, sco, par` (3rd of A–F)
before the group stage is even over.
**Impact:** wrong "alive" count on the leaderboard; a team that actually qualifies
shows dead permanently. **Fix:** only auto-eliminate a team that is *certainly*
out — `rank === 4` (4th of 4 never qualifies), or any non-advanced team **once all
groups are complete**. Leaves 3rd-placed alive-pending until the picture is final.

## IMPROVEMENTS / OPTIMISATIONS
- I1 — **Read amplification on every load.** `autoSyncFromESPN` loops the other
  sweeps and `autoSyncStandings` loops *all* known sweeps, each doing
  `loadById` (+ `saveSweep` only if changed). On a device with K sweeps that's
  ~2K Supabase reads on every app open, even at steady state. Fine at
  friends/family scale (free tier, few sweeps); revisit if it grows.
  ([autoSyncStandings](src/App.jsx:923))
- I2 — **CJS `exports.handler` in a `"type":"module"` package** triggers an
  esbuild warning for both `fixtures.js` and `standings.js`. Netlify bundles them
  fine (fixtures runs in prod), so cosmetic — but converting both to ESM `export`
  or `.cjs` would remove a latent footgun. Don't churn now.

  *completed* groups, rank 1 is final once a group is done. No premature/sticky
